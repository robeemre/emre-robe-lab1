Transportation in Toronto

With a population of over 2 million people, Toronto is Canada's largest city. As a result, it needs a comprehensive transportation network to accommodate its urban and broader metropolitan population. Toronto's transportation facilities enable commuters to travel by public transit on its bus, streetcar and subway lines, or by car on its roads and highways.
